Brian Williams

My name is Brian Williams and I am very interested in the recently vacated position on the Sallisaw School Board. I am a Christian, a Cherokee Indian, and a lifelong Sallisaw resident who graduated from Sallisaw High School in 1998. My wife Christa Williams and I have 3 children; Braxton (8), Holden (6), & Lauren (1) who currently attend or will be attending Sallisaw Schools. As a father, I see this opportunity as a way to improve the future for my children; and that is not something that I take lightly. I feel that I am uniquely qualified to offer something different to our school board. I have almost 20 years of professional experience in the Information Technology industry and currently serve as a Software Systems Principal Engineer at Dell, Inc. My work days consist of full stack software development and data science for some of the largest enterprise companies in the world.
